- the day's weather
- current time
- Upcoming rocket launches from companies like SpaceX, NASA etc
- any flights that are going above us (from the local flightradar antenna setup)
- upcoming schedules for kids classes and extracurricular activities (from the google calendar)

Of these things, there are some things that i can add over a period of time - and they can be dropped from the build sequence. We can get back to it later on. 

Over time I think it should also extend a bit to add more information that is relevant and might also change depending on the time of the day etc. 

BTW, specifcially about the weather: The weather part should be animated(exp, if its raining the background has rain, The rain should also bounce of off the other widgets on the page.
